About Me

Where design
meets impact.

I'm an Interaction Design student from Delhi who turns ideas into experiences. I work across the full creative spectrum — from concept sketching and social strategy to motion design and AI-powered content workflows.

I've shaped visual communication for some of India's biggest institutions — Delhi Metro (DMRC), Ministry of Mines, DDA, Namami Gange — and built social ecosystems for startups from the ground up.

I believe the best design is invisible — it moves people before they realise they've been moved.
